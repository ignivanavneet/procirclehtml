WHY JOIN US?

Tired of having to go through recruiters? No time to trawl through job boards? LinkedIn not working out for you? Join our Professional network for FREE and connect with hundreds of potential employers looking for people just like you to join their team.

THE PROCIRCLE VISION

People are not one dimensional, every professional has a unique personality. In order to create optimal matches we have integrated the Procircle personality questionnaire into our platform. This way you will be matched with employers not only based on your qualifications but your personality and values too. We believe job boards are old-fashioned. Thats why weve added the human element to job-searching – people talking to people! On Procircle, you will only ever talk to employers that are genuinely interested in YOU!

WHY JOIN US?

We’ve taken all the hard work out of the job searching process for you.
Our system suggests potential matches with employers who want to hear from professionals with your background and personality.

You also have the option to use our advanced search function if you would like to take a more proactive approach. You can choose to ignore the suggested matches and send a match request to whoever you like. If both sides agree to a match, go ahead, you can start communicating with each other!
You can make up to 30 different matches per month!
